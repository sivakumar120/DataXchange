In the ever expanding data market DataXchange is positioned at the crossroads between data providers and data users, starting from an expressed need of data-driven leadership.
Founder's Vision
We have founded DataXchange to harness the power of data. We believe there is a growing need for companies to procure data sets to help drive growth through better market visibility, AI algorithms to improve customer knowledge, operational efficiency or reduce costs. We want to become the “trusted data monetisation partner” between data users and data providers, helping the overall market to become more data driven.

DataXchange strives to become the trusted partner for the monetisation of your data.
